### Memoary — Interactive Digital Yearbook

A React-based digital yearbook viewer built around a realistic **3D page-flip book interface** — every turn uses spring physics, cast shadows, and simulated light to feel like real paper, not a slideshow.

- 🎬 Realistic 3D page-flip animation powered by GSAP & Framer Motion spring physics
- 💡 Custom light simulation with cast shadows and spine shadows for a tactile feel
- 🎨 Editorial visual identity — `Cormorant Garamond` + `DM Sans`, warm parchment palette
- 🗂️ Structured admin dashboard built on its own mini design system
